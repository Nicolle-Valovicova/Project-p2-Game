Easter Eggs & Hidden Interactions
------------------------------------

1. The Legacy Crow
In the Legacy menu, a tiny crow is perched quietly… until you click on it.
Give it a tap and it will let out a soft crow, acknowledging your discovery.

2. Developer Trigger # White Victory Screen
Clicking 7x on the White King crow instantly triggers the White team’s winning screen.
This feature was originally made for testing and demonstrating the features in the game for the school project.

3. Developer Trigger # Black Victory Screen
The Black King crow works the same way, one click launches the Black team’s victory ending.

4.Secret Lore 
Inside the Legacy section, one sentence hides a secret.
Hover over the final line and the website will reveal a deeper story
a hidden tale that expands the world behind Murder’s Gambit.
