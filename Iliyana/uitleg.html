Rules:

  • You must complete each dish by tapping the steps in the correct order.
  • You start with 3 lives.
  • Wrong step? You lose 1 life and must redo the same dish.
  • You move to the next level when you finish all dishes with at least 1 life left.
  • You win the game if you complete all levels without losing all your lives.
  • You lose the game if you reach 0 lives or make too many mistakes.
